Straitrunner: Hormuz

Open the Strait of Hormuz · iPhone & Android

In February 2026 the Strait of Hormuz closed. Mines went into the water, two thousand ships were trapped, and a fifth of the world's oil stopped moving. This game is about opening it back up.

A minesweeper with a different goal

Classic minesweeper logic, one big twist: you win by connecting the Persian Gulf to the Gulf of Oman with a continuous channel of swept hexes, not by clearing the board. Hitting a mine leaves that hex impassable, blocking your channel and stacking tankers at the approaches. Seven chapters follow the real timeline, from the first rumor of mines in July 2025 to the July 2026 re-escalation, with dated, paraphrased headlines.

Completely free

The full campaign is free. No ads, no accounts, no data collection. A passion project from a lone developer who loves strategy, geopolitics, and old-school minesweeper.
