How to play

  • Goal: connect the Persian Gulf (west) to the Gulf of Oman (east) with a continuous channel of swept hexes. You do not need to clear the whole board.
  • Tap a hex to sweep it. Your first tap is always safe.
  • Hold a hex to flag a suspected mine.
  • Tap a revealed number whose flags match it to sweep the rest of its neighbors at once.
  • Hitting a mine leaves the hex impassable and adds tankers to the backlog. If the backlog fills, the strait closes and the level fails.
  • One late chapter has two shipping lanes (open both), and the last chapters have active minelayers seeding new mines as tankers stack up.

Frequently asked

The game is sideways. It's landscape-only, like the strait itself. Rotate your phone and disable rotation lock.

I hear no sound. Tap the speaker icon in the top bar; also check the silent switch and volume.

My progress disappeared. Progress is stored on the device. Deleting the app deletes progress; there is no cloud sync in v1.

Is this game taking a side in the conflict? No. The chapters follow the reported public record of the 2026 closure of the Strait of Hormuz, with headlines paraphrased from statements by the parties and international organizations. The full campaign is free by design.
