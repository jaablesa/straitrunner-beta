Privacy Policy

Effective July 11, 2026 · Developer: José Abreu, publishing as an individual

The short version

Straitrunner: Hormuz collects no personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, no tracking, and the game makes no network requests while you play.

What the app stores on your device

Your game progress (chapters cleared, best times, sound preference, tutorial state) is stored locally on your device using standard app storage. It never leaves your device, and we cannot see it. Deleting the app deletes it.

What we collect

Nothing. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information, device identifiers, usage analytics, or diagnostics.

Purchases

If a future version offers optional content, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play) under their payment terms and privacy policies. We receive no payment details, and no purchase information that identifies you personally.

Children

Straitrunner: Hormuz is not directed at children. It contains references to a real armed conflict presented in a documentary style and carries the age rating shown on its store listing. Because we collect no data from anyone, we collect no data from children.

Third parties

The app contains no third-party SDKs, ad networks, or analytics tools.

Changes

If a future update changes any of the above (for example, adding online leaderboards), we will update this policy and the app's store privacy labels before that update ships.
